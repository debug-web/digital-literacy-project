# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a positive and professional profile  
- Think before posting anything online  
- Use privacy settings to protect personal information  
- Share useful and meaningful content  
- Respect other's opinions and communicate politely  

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in online arguments or negativity  
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information  
- Do not misuse other's content without permission
