# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Always verify the identity of unknown callers or messages  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions  

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety
- Never scan a QR code to receive money  
- Always double-check the payment request before approving  
- Do not install unknown apps suggested by strangers  
- Use only official banking and payment apps  

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
